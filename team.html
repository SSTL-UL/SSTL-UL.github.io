Dr. Bobin Wang

Assistant Professor

Department of mechanical engineering and industrial engineering

Principal Investigator

Bobin Wang, Ph.D., is a professor of industrial engineering in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at Université Laval. She holds a renowned PhD in transportation engineering and has over 10 years of research experience in mobility analysis. Her work covers various fields, including transportation planning, environmental studies, smart cities, and intelligent transportation systems, with a particular focus on the relationship between transportation and climate change, the interrelationships between transportation, land use planning, and the environment, integrated urban mobility (MaaS), transportation network analysis, transportation simulation, the interaction between AI and decision modeling, and data science. She is an emerging researcher; her advantage in multidisciplinary research can help generate collaborations to optimize capabilities and foster innovation.
