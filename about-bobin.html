Prof. Bobin Wang

Dr. Bobin Wang

Assistant Professor

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Principal Investigator

Department of Mechanical Engineering, Université Laval.

Bobin Wang, Ph.D., is a professor of industrial engineering in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at Université Laval. She holds a renowned PhD in transportation engineering and has over 10 years of research experience in mobility analysis. Her work covers various fields, including transportation planning, environmental studies, smart cities, and intelligent transportation systems.

She has extensive research and work experience across China, Europe, and North America, with more than 50 publications and 2 monographs in leading transportation journals. She is the principal investigator of several national and provincial projects in Canada supported by NSERC and FRQNT. Her future research will focus on climate change, smart cities, and intelligent transportation systems.

Research Interests

Her research focuses on the relationship between transportation and climate change, the interrelationships between transportation, land use planning, and the environment, integrated urban mobility (MaaS), transportation network analysis, transportation simulation, the interaction between AI and decision modeling, and data science.
