Graduate Studies at SSTL

We are always looking for passionate and talented students to join our research group. If you are interested in shaping the future of mobility, we encourage you to explore the opportunities below.

Why Join SSTL?

As a member of the Smart & Sustainable Transportation Lab, you will be part of a collaborative and innovative research environment. You will have the opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects with real-world impact, publish in top-tier academic journals, and present your work at international conferences. Our lab provides access to state-of-the-art computational resources and unique datasets.

Open Positions

We are actively recruiting for the following positions. All positions are fully funded.

Ph.D. Positions

We are seeking exceptional Ph.D. candidates with strong quantitative and programming skills. Ideal candidates will have a background in transportation engineering, civil engineering, computer science, operations research, or a related field. Research topics are flexible but generally fall within our core research areas.

M.Sc. Positions

We welcome applications from motivated students for our Master of Science (M.Sc.) program. M.Sc. students will work closely with Prof. Wang and senior lab members on a focused research project, culminating in a thesis. This is an excellent opportunity to gain in-depth research experience in preparation for a career in industry or further doctoral studies.

How to Apply

Interested candidates are encouraged to follow the official application process for graduate studies at Université Laval. Additionally, please send the following documents directly to Prof. Bobin Wang via email at bobin.wang@gmc.ulaval.ca:

  • A cover letter outlining your research interests and why you are a good fit for our lab.
  • Your detailed Curriculum Vitae (CV).
  • Transcripts from all post-secondary institutions attended.
  • A sample of your academic writing (e.g., a published paper or a course project report).

Please note that due to the high volume of applications, only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
