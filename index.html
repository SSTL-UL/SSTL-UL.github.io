Meet the Team

Our Mission

The Smart & Sustainable Transportation Lab (SSTL) is dedicated to advancing research and developing innovative solutions for modern mobility challenges. Our work focuses on the intersection of transportation engineering, artificial intelligence, and sustainable urban planning.

Our Approach

Our research integrates theoretical insights with practical, data-driven decision modeling. We specialize in several key areas:

  • Analysis of travel behavior and discrete choice modeling.
  • Development of integrated urban mobility solutions like Mobility as a Service (MaaS).
  • Examination of the relationship between transportation, climate change, and land use.
  • Application of AI, machine learning, and econometric modeling to solve transportation problems.
