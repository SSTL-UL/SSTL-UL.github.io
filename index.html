Meet the Team

Mission

The Smart & Sustainable Transportation Lab (SSTL) / Laboratoire de transport intelligent et durable (LTID) is dedicated to advancing research and developing innovative solutions for modern mobility challenges. Our work focuses on the cutting-edge interdisciplinary research spanning transportation and industrial engineering (human mobility and logistics), social science (transportation and land use), computer science (intelligent transportation systems), environmental engineering (transportation and climate change), and health-related outcomes (transportation and well-being).

Research

Our research integrates theoretical insights with practical, data-driven decision modeling. We specialize in several key areas:

  • Analysis of human mobility, vehicle flows, and freight logistics in multimodal transportation networks.
  • Development of integrated urban mobility solutions including Mobility as a Service (MaaS).
  • Examination of the interrelationships among transportation systems, environmental impacts, energy use, and land-use patterns.
  • Application of AI, machine learning, econometric, and behavioral–psychological models for transportation systems analysis and decision-making.
  • Design of stated- and revealed-preference (SP/RP) surveys and experiments for behavioral modeling and policy analysis.
