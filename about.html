Professor

Prof. Bobin Wang

Assistant Professor

Prof. Wang is an assistant professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Université Laval. She holds a Ph.D. in Traffic and Transportation Planning and Management. Her research focuses on travel behavior analysis, transportation and climate change, transportation network analysis, the interaction of AI and decision-making theories, and econometric and statistical modelling. She has extensive research and work experience across China, Europe, and North America, with more than 50 publications and 2 monographs in leading transportation journals. She is the principal investigator of several national and provincial projects in Canada supported by NSERC and FRQNT. Her future research will focus on climate change, smart cities, and intelligent transportation systems.
